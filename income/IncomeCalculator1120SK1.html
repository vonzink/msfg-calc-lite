Schedule K-1 (1120S) Guidelines

S-Corporation K-1 income represents the shareholder's pro-rata share of corporate income. Unlike partnership K-1s, there are no guaranteed payments. S-Corp shareholders receive compensation via W-2 wages. Include ordinary business income/loss (Line 1) and net rental real estate income/loss (Lines 2-3).
