How It Calculates

  • Points: Positive points = cost to borrower (price down), negative points = credit to borrower (price up)
  • Final Price: Par (100.000) minus final points
  • Dollar Impact: Loan amount × (final points ÷ 100)
  • Combos: Some adjustments stack (e.g., High-Balance ARM gets additional adjustment)
